2. The function of a commutator in a DC machine is to:

Reduce eddy current losses
Convert DC to AC
Convert AC to DC
Increase the current
Explanation: The commutator converts the internally generated AC in the armature to DC at the output.

3. The torque developed in a 3-phase induction motor is maximum when:

Rotor resistance = rotor reactance
Rotor reactance = stator reactance
Rotor resistance = stator resistance
Slip is zero
Explanation: Torque is maximum when rotor resistance equals rotor reactance at the operating frequency.

4. A synchronous motor runs at:

Below synchronous speed
Above synchronous speed
Synchronous speed
Variable speed
Explanation: A synchronous motor runs at constant synchronous speed regardless of load.

5. The efficiency of a transformer is maximum when:

Load is full
Load is half
Core loss = copper loss
Copper loss is zero
Explanation: Transformer efficiency is maximum when iron losses equal copper losses.

6. The no-load current of a transformer has:

High magnitude and low power factor
Low magnitude and low power factor
High magnitude and high power factor
Low magnitude and high power factor
Explanation: No-load current is small and highly lagging due to magnetizing inductance.

7. The speed of a DC shunt motor is:

Constant
Inversely proportional to armature voltage
Proportional to armature current
Inversely proportional to field current
Explanation: Speed ∝ 1/Φ and Φ depends on field current. So speed increases when field current decreases.

8. The starting torque of a squirrel cage induction motor is:

Low
High
Very high
Zero
Explanation: Squirrel cage motors have low starting torque but are simple and rugged.

9. In transformer core, silicon is added to:

Increase conductivity
Increase permeability
Reduce eddy current losses
Reduce weight
Explanation: Silicon increases resistivity of core material, reducing eddy current losses.

10. Which of the following machines has the highest starting torque?

DC shunt motor
DC series motor
Induction motor
Universal motor
Explanation: DC series motors produce very high starting torque due to series connection of field and armature.
